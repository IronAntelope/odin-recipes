Mexican Veges and Rice

  • Serves: 3
  • Prep time: 20 mins
  • Cook time: 40 mins

Description

A tasty yet healthy, mexican inspired vegan dish. Satiating and rich in flavour.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brown rice
  • Firm tofu, diced
  • 1/2 spanish onion, jullienne
  • 1 small to medium mushroom, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 Red capsicum, diced
  • 1/2 cup of corn
  • 1/2 lime
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced into wedges

Instructions

  1. Add one cup of brown rice to a saucepan with three cups of water. Heat on medium-high flame. It will start to boil over after about 15 mins (not to worry, you can clean later).
  2. While the rice is cooking, there is about 25 minutets spare to cut up all the veges.

The order in which you cook the veges isn't that important, however, the follow instructions are a good guide.

  1. With about 10 minutes left on the rice, heat a non-stick pan on medium-high flame. First cook the tofu for 5 minutes or until browned (you can take the tofu out and add it back in later or leave it in).
  2. Add the sliced mushroom, and stir occasionally.
  3. Once mushrooms are soft, add the onion, corn and capsicum. Stir constantly and season generously with pepper. You can add a bit of water to get a sizzle and really cook the pepper through.
  4. With rice and veges both cooked now, serve the rice first and top with the veges and avocado wedges. Garnish with squeezed lime juice and enjoy!
